MISSION

Fiber-optic drones operate without radio emissions. They cannot be detected, classified, or defeated by RF-centric counter-UAS systems. ACU Labs treats this gap as the next operational frontier.

  • ■ PASSIVE DETECTION. Acoustic arrays identify rotor and propulsion signatures with no transmitted energy.
  • ■ MULTI-NODE LOCALIZATION. Distributed sensor geometry resolves bearing and range without depending on the threat’s emissions.
  • ■ RESPONSIBLE R&D. All demonstrations are software-simulated. No live emitters, no operational parameters published.

TECHNOLOGY

FIBERSENTRY ACOUSTIC

FiberSentry Acoustic is our research platform for fiber-optic UAV detection. The system combines distributed acoustic sensing, passive multi-node geometry, and signature-driven classification.

The current build is a simulation-only demonstration intended for investor and partner briefings. No live transmitters, no operational tuning, no countermeasure parameters.
